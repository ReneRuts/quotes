# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Quote Bot ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot service.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use Quote Bot, we automatically collect and store the following information:

- **Server Information**: Discord server (guild) ID, server name
- **Channel Information**: Channel IDs where the bot is configured to send quotes
- **User Information**: User IDs for users who interact with the bot (e.g., saving favorite quotes)
- **Configuration Data**: Timezone, quote delivery time, interval settings, and role mentions
- **Interaction Data**: Reactions to quotes (for the favorites feature)

### Information You Provide

- **Favorite Quotes**: When you react with ❤️ to save a quote, we store your user ID and the associated quote

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

- Deliver daily motivational quotes to your configured Discord channels
- Maintain your bot configuration settings (schedule, timezone, channel preferences)
- Store and retrieve your favorite quotes
- Improve and maintain the bot's functionality
- Debug technical issues

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored in a local SQLite database (`quotes.db`)
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data
- No data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes
- Server and user data is automatically deleted when the bot is removed from a server

## Third-Party Services

Quote Bot integrates with the following third-party services:

### Discord
- Our bot operates on Discord's platform and is subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)
- We only access information necessary for bot functionality through Discord's API

### ZenQuotes API
- Quotes are fetched from [ZenQuotes.io](https://zenquotes.io/)
- We do not share any user or server information with ZenQuotes
- ZenQuotes' data collection is governed by their own privacy policy

## Data Retention

- **Configuration Data**: Retained while the bot remains in your server
- **Favorite Quotes**: Retained until you remove them or the bot is removed from the server
- **Deleted Data**: When the bot is removed from a server, all associated configuration data is automatically deleted

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request information about the data we store about you or your server
- **Deletion**: Remove the bot from your server to delete all associated data
- **Correction**: Update your configuration settings at any time using the `/setup` command

## Children's Privacy

Quote Bot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Data Deletion

To delete your data:

1. **Server Data**: Remove the bot from your server to automatically delete all server configuration data
2. **Personal Favorites**: Your favorites are deleted when the bot is removed from servers where you saved them
3. **Manual Request**: Contact us at the support server for manual data deletion requests
